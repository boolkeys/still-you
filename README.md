# Still You

> *Don't let AI think for you.*

**Still You** is a movement for cognitive sovereignty in the age of universal intelligence. It encourages every human to build a personal context file, a documented identity that captures how *you* actually think, and plug it into whatever AI you use.

Platform-agnostic. Open source. Sponsored by [AskMojo](https://askmojo.ai).

→ Movement landing : [askmojo.ai/still-you](https://askmojo.ai/still-you)

## Why this exists

Large language models are not born neutral. After training on the open internet, they go through a phase of **reinforcement learning from human feedback** (RLHF) where a small group of human reviewers, mostly based in a single country and shaped by a narrow set of cultural defaults, labels which answers count as "good", "safe", and "appropriate".

Their preferences become the model's defaults. Then those defaults are served back to billions of people as if they were neutral common ground. The slow, invisible alignment of eight billion minds onto one default is what this movement calls **The Flattening**.

The response is counter-intuitive : not to hide your thinking, but to *document* it. Deliberately. In writing. So that what the machine produces matches what is in your head, instead of replacing it with the median of someone else's review pool.

A personal context file is the simplest tool for that.
